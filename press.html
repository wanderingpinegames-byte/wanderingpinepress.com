About Wandering Pine Press

Wandering Pine Press is an independent tabletop RPG publisher focused on narrative-first games that are easy to start and designed to fit into real, busy lives. Founded by husband and wife team Brian and Crystal Hazzard in Virginia.

One-line description:

Wandering Pine Press is an independent publisher with a focus on games that help players collaborate to tell compelling character-driven stories.

Short bio (for show notes, introductions, etc.):

Wandering Pine Press is an independent tabletop RPG publisher from Virginia. Founded by designer Brian Hazzard and editor/layout designer Crystal Hazzard, the company focuses on narrative-first games that are easy to learn and designed to fit into real, busy lives. Their catalog includes full campaign games and the One Night Worlds line of single-session adventures.

Designer Bio — Brian Hazzard

Short bio (for podcast intros):

Brian Hazzard is the designer and creative director of Wandering Pine Press. He designs narrative-first tabletop RPGs built around character-driven storytelling and accessible mechanics. A lifelong collector of hobbies — boardgames, gamebooks, RPGs, papercraft, and magic tricks — Brian is always looking for the next great obsession.

Long bio (for written features):

Brian Hazzard is the designer and creative director behind Wandering Pine Press, an independent tabletop RPG publisher based in Virginia. Brian's games prioritize narrative over combat, accessibility over complexity, and character over crunch. His work includes the sci-fi TTRPG Terminus X7Z, the 1920s vigilante noir game In Alleys Dark, and the growing One Night Worlds line of single-session adventures spanning fantasy, steampunk, and more. Brian lives in Virginia with his wife Crystal, their two sons, and two very lazy cats.

Game Descriptions

Terminus X7Z: A Latticeverse Game

Full Campaign

A sci-fi TTRPG inspired by shows like Star Trek, built around diplomacy, culture clash, and coexistence among the stars. In a future where humanity shares the galaxy with dozens of alien species, combat is rarely the answer — the real drama happens at the negotiating table.

In Alleys Dark: A Rankston Reckoning

Full Campaign

A 1920s vigilante noir TTRPG set in the corrupt city of Rankston. Players take on the role of a band of vigilantes — ordinary people who've decided that enough is enough — navigating a city where the law looks the other way and every decision carries moral weight.

One Night Worlds: The Fires of Chaterra

One Night World

A whimsical fairytale one-shot adventure featuring a sleeping king, a stolen throne, mushroom folk with ancient secrets, and a wicked witch with a very bad plan. Designed to run in a single session with no prep required.

One Night Worlds: Tales of Clockwork & Chaos

One Night World

A steampunk one-shot about resistance and revolution. The Tocks — clockwork rulers — control the city. You're human, and you've had enough. A story about ingenuity, solidarity, and the cost of fighting for freedom.

One Night Worlds: Terminus X7Z

One Night World

A self-contained single-session version of Terminus X7Z. All the interspecies intrigue of the Latticeverse, distilled into one night of play. Perfect for new players or groups who want something self-contained.

One Night Worlds: In Alleys Dark

One Night World

A complete vigilante noir adventure in a single session. Fog-drenched streets, moral choices, and shadowy figures — no prep, no long commitment, all atmosphere.
