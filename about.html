We're the Hazzards

We're the husband and wife team behind Wandering Pine Press. We live in Virginia with two wild sons, two lazy cats, and a lot of love. As an independent tabletop RPG publisher, we focus on games that help players collaborate to tell compelling character-driven stories. Thank you for taking an interest in our craft.

What We Stand For

We Believe Tabletop RPGs Should…

📖

Be Easy to Start

No 300-page rulebooks. No weeks of prep. Wandering Pine Press games are designed so you can open the box, gather your friends, and play.

🎭

Tell Meaningful Stories

Mechanics should serve the story, not the other way around. We build systems that ask interesting questions and then get out of the way.

⏱️

Fit Into Real, Busy Lives

From full campaigns to single-session adventures, every game we make is designed around the reality that your time is precious.
