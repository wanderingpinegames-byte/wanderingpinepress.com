Terminus X7Z

A Latticeverse Game — Navigate a future where humanity shares the stars, and tensions run higher than warp speed.

About the Game

Terminus X7Z is a sci-fi tabletop RPG inspired by shows like Star Trek — built around diplomacy, culture clash, and the complicated, often messy business of coexistence. In a future where humanity is one species among dozens, combat is rarely the right answer. The real drama happens at the negotiating table, in the corridors of a shared station, in the moment when two cultures misunderstand each other in ways that could start a war.

What's In the Box

  • A complete rulebook with everything you need to play
  • Pre-generated characters to get started fast

Who Is This Game For?

If your table loves character-driven stories, enjoys political intrigue more than dungeon crawling, or just wants to boldly go somewhere new — Terminus X7Z was made for you.
